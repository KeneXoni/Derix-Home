Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here you will find answers to the most popular questions about the DERIX Home service. If you didn’t find the answer — use the chatbot.

What is DERIX Home?

DERIX Home is a service for selecting, renting, and selling real estate, helping you find an apartment or house according to your needs.

How to find housing with DERIX Home?

You can use the DERIX Home chatbot, which will help you choose housing by district, budget, and type of property.

How does the DERIX Home chatbot work?

The chatbot works in Telegram and is available 24/7. To start a dialogue, click the “Chat with DERIX” button in the bottom right corner of the page and select the desired action.

Can I rent out or sell my property?

Yes, through the chatbot or the feedback form you can leave information about your property and contact an agent.

How to contact an agent?

To contact an agent, use the corresponding button in the chatbot or fill out the form on the “Contacts” page.
